About the Program

Educating the Educators of Science and Mathematics

The BS Mathematics and Science Teaching (BSMST) program combines rigorous training in mathematics and natural science content with a strong foundation in pedagogy, educational psychology, and field-based teaching practice, producing graduates who are ready to lead classrooms and communities.

1

Strong Content Foundation

Deep coursework in mathematics, biology, chemistry, and physics ensures graduates can teach with mastery and confidence.

2

Research-Based Pedagogy

Courses in educational theory, assessment, and curriculum design are grounded in current research on how students learn.

3

Field-Based Practice

Supervised practice teaching in partner schools develops classroom-ready skills before graduation.

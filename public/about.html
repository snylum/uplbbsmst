About BS Mathematics and Science Teaching

The program is offered by the College of Arts and Sciences of the University of the Philippines Los Baños, drawing on the College's strength in the natural sciences and mathematics to prepare well-rounded secondary school teachers.

Mission

To produce competent, innovative, and socially responsible teachers of mathematics and science who can address the learning needs of Filipino secondary students.

Vision

To be a recognized center of excellence in mathematics and science teacher education in the Philippines and the Southeast Asian region.

Program Objectives

Graduates of the BSMST program are expected to:

Demonstrate mastery of core concepts in mathematics and the natural sciences.

Apply research-based pedagogical strategies suited to diverse learners.

Design and implement standards-based instructional materials and assessments.

Engage in reflective practice and continuing professional development.

Uphold professional and ethical standards of the teaching profession.

Contribute to community and national development through science and mathematics literacy.

Program History

The BS Mathematics and Science Teaching program was established by the College of Arts and Sciences to respond to the national need for qualified secondary school mathematics and science teachers, combining the College's disciplinal expertise with professional education coursework.

Admissions

Admission Requirements

Admission to the BSMST program follows University of the Philippines system-wide undergraduate admission policies, with additional program-specific requirements for shiftees and transferees.

Freshman Applicants

Apply through the UP College Admission Test (UPCAT). Qualified examinees may indicate UPLB and the BSMST program as a choice during application.

Visit UPCAT Portal

Shiftees & Transferees

Currently enrolled UP students and transferees from other institutions may apply through the CAS shifting/transfer process, subject to slot availability and academic standing requirements.
